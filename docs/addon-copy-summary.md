## Psychological Triggers Used

### üéØ Conversion Principles Applied

1. **FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)**
   - "Limited to 2 BBQ setups per day"
   - "Books out 2-3 months in advance"
   - "Weather-dependent - book early"

2. **Value Anchoring**
   - "$65 vs $200+ to buy equipment"
   - "Same seafood costs $250+ at Noosa restaurants"
   - "Less than hotel wifi charges"

3. **Social Proof**
   - Authentic testimonials from real locations
   - Specific details (ages, catch counts, locations)
   - 5-star experiences implied

4. **Emotional Connection**
   - Memory preservation ("years from now")
   - Family bonding moments
   - Once-in-a-lifetime experiences

5. **Exclusivity**
   - "95% of tourists never see"
   - "VIP treatment"
   - "Secret beaches"

6. **Regret Avoidance**
   - "Biggest regret was not capturing properly"
   - "Memories fade - photos don't"
   - "Don't settle for ordinary"

7. **Specificity**
   - Exact equipment specs (4K, 100+ Mbps, 128GB)
   - Precise timing (48 hours, 3 weeks)
   - Real fish species, locations, features
